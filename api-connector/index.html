FAQ

You've got questions. We've got answers.

Does Syncd replace Fivetran / Airbyte / Stitch?

No — it sits alongside them. Syncd watches the APIs your existing connectors pull from and patches the connector config when schemas change. Think of it as a co-pilot for your ingestion layer, not a replacement.

What SaaS APIs do you support?

We monitor 200+ APIs out of the box including Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, Netsuite, Zendesk, Shopify, Marketo, Workday, and most major CRM, marketing, and finance platforms. Custom API support is available on Enterprise.

How do auto-fixes work? Are they safe?

Syncd never ships a fix without validation. Every proposed remap runs against a snapshot of your downstream models. If the change would break anything, Syncd flags it for human review instead. You control the approval threshold.

What happens when Syncd can't auto-fix something?

You get a detailed alert with the exact change, which fields are affected, which downstream models are at risk, and a suggested manual fix. On Pro and Enterprise, you can loop in our support team for same-day help.

Is there a long-term contract?

Starter and Pro are month-to-month. Cancel anytime. Enterprise plans are annual with custom terms.
